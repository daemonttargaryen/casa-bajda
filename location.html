Finding Us

Location

Marsaskala is on Malta's quiet east coast — well placed for the whole island, away from the tourist crowds, and five minutes' walk from the sea.

Address

We're in Marsaskala.

Marsaskala is one of Malta's most authentic villages — a working fishing community on the south-east coast. It's not a tourist resort, and that's exactly the point. You'll find real restaurants, a calm bay, and the feeling of actual Maltese life.

We're on Triq il-Baħar, the main seafront street, one minute from the promenade and two minutes from the best pastizzi shop in the village.

Getting Here

How to reach us.

✈
From the Airport
Malta International Airport is 20 minutes by car. We offer a flat-rate transfer for €20 — just let us know your flight details when you book.
🚌
By Bus
Bus routes 71 and 72 connect Marsaskala to Valletta. The bus stop is a 3-minute walk from us. Journey to Valletta takes about 45 minutes.
🚗
By Car
We have free private parking behind the guesthouse. Coming from the airport, take the arterial road towards Żejtun, then follow signs for Marsaskala.

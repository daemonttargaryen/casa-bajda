Our Story

A real Maltese home, open to the world.

Casa Bajda — bajda means white in Maltese — has stood on the waterfront in Marsaskala for over a hundred years. The Borg family has lived here for three generations. We opened the doors to guests in 2009 and have been welcoming people from all over the world ever since.

This isn't a hotel. It's our home, and we treat every guest accordingly. You'll get local advice, a proper breakfast, and someone who genuinely cares whether you have a good time in Malta.
